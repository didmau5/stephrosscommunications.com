About Stephanie
Communications Specialist

With an educational background in public relations and professional experience in corporate communications, Stephanie has experience writing advertising and website copy, corporate biographies, blog posts, press releases, newspaper articles and email and newsletter content, as well as managing social media platforms for small to medium size businesses in Vancouver, BC. As an experienced communications professional with a demonstrated history of content management for a variety of industries, Stephanie specializes in corporate communications and writing in various forms. Originally from Vancouver Island, Stephanie has lived her entire life in the pacific northwest and currently calls Vancouver, BC home. As an avid traveller, Stephanie has had the pleasure of exploring multiple continents and just under a dozen countries. Stay tuned for her travel blog, chronicling her adventures.
